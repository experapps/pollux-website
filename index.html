Authorization Matrix

Pollux gives you the power to establish appropriate checks and balances in place, to ensure a smooth and secure experience, by enforcing a dynamic maker-checker path for your transaction – as per the internal policy of your organisation. The authorization matrix can be customized to suit your company’s needs. Read More

Integration Capabilities

Integration with various third-party software is one of the most important aspect of any payment platform. Pollux pay takes payment instructions from your ERP, with added level of security using OTP and Two factor Authentication, and ensures you enjoy a seamless transaction flow. Read More
