Features

Authorization Matrix

Pollux gives you the power to establish appropriate checks and balances in place, to ensure a smooth and secure experience, by enforcing a dynamic maker-checker path for your transaction – as per the internal policy of your organisation. The authorization matrix can be customized to suit your company’s needs. More

Integration Capabilities

Integration with various third-party software is one of the most important aspect of any payment platform. Pollux pay takes payment instructions from your ERP, with added level of security using OTP and Two factor Authentication, and ensures you enjoy a seamless transaction flow. More

Smart Transaction Analytics

Data runs businesses. Dissect your payments and create insightful reports with our powerful report builder to give you useful transaction data to act upon. More

Seamless Auditing Capabilities

Pollux Pay generates appropriate transaction logs and data to assist Corporate Audits sufficiently and appropriately. More
