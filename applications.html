Vendor Payments

Pollux Pay enables Corporates to pay their Vendors, whether one at a time, or bulk payments, with ease, directly through the Commercial Card. The Vendor Payment Module further enables the Corporate User to create Groups for assigning tasks, Rules to enforce internal policy, and certain other enhanced controls, checks and balances to implement Purchase, Finance and Accounts Policy of the Corporate. Appropriate OTP Triggers, Maker-Checker Authorisation Matrices, Session Tracking, among other security features, have been implemented to ensure that the payments made are secure. The Vendor Payments Module has 3 major functions:

  • Registration of Vendors
  • Payment to Vendors
  • Generating Payment Reports

Utility Bill Payments

Through Pollux Pay, Corporates can directly make payments for their Utility Bills in the nature of Cellular Payments, Electricity Bills, Insurance Payments, among others, with a whole range of Billers on the platform. Managing such recurring payments by creating automated workflows, Groups and Rules becomes easier using Pollux Pay. The following kinds of utility payments can be made through the platform:

  • Electricity Bills (73 Billers)
  • Insurance Payments (23 Billers)
  • Gas Connection Bills (18 Billers)
  • Broadband Bills (13 Billers)
  • Mobile Bills (9 Billers)
  • Mobile Recharges (9 Billers)
  • Telephone Bills (6 Billers)

Travel Cards

The Digital Portal offered by Pollux Pay provides a complete digital experience for Travel Cards (Forex Card) Customers of the Bank to perform a host of activities seamlessly on the go. With Group Limits and Rules, tracking and managing Forex Cards, and loading of balance onto the cards becomes easy. The following activities are supported by Pollux Pay:

  • Applying for Forex Travel Cards
  • Reloading Existing Forex Travel Cards
  • Encashing Existing Forex Travel Cards

GST Payments

Making GST Payments with Pollux Pay is hassle free and easy. It accommodates payment of unitary payments as well as bulk payments, directly through the Issued Commercial Cards. Moreover, the authorisation matrix implements the Maker-Checker concept and an added layer of security to ensure integrity of payments.


Direct Tax Payments

Direct Tax, TDS and TCS Payments can be made seamlessly with Pollux Pay, whether it is unitary payments or bulk payments, directly through the Commercial Cards issued by Pollux Pay. Moreover, the authorisation matrix implements the Maker-Checker concept and an added layer of security to ensure integrity of payments.
