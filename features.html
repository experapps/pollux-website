Features


Integration Capabilities

Integration with various third-party software is one of the most important aspects of any payment platform. Pollux Pay takes payment instructions from your ERP, with added level of security using OTP and Two factor Authentication, and ensures you enjoy a seamless transaction flow. It also offers Host to Host integration solutions for corporates to connect their accounting or ERP system with Pollux. This enables users to carry out transactions through SAP Utility, SFTP Utility, Snorkel Middleware or through Web Services with minimal intervention.

Authorisation Matrix

Pollux gives you the power to establish appropriate checks and balances in place to ensure a smooth and secure experience by enforcing a dynamic maker-checker path for your transaction – as per the internal policy of your organisation. The authorisation matrix can be customised to suit your company’s needs. It ensures greater security, checks and balances through the customisable matrix. Further, segregation of users into Groups, and setting of additional Rules enhances security and internal control.

Instant Notifications

Instant notifications are sent through SMS and e-mail to the users at various activity levels, from logins, vendor and user registration to payment initiation. Pollux Pay affords you the flexibility to set notifications for each activity – or receive them only for the most important ones. You can further use the dynamic Notification Centre to set and keep track of the activities happening in your portal. Moreover, adhoc as well as regular payments to vendors triggers a "Payment Advice" that is sent to the vendor confirming the funds transfer.


Reports and Analytics

Data runs businesses. Understand your business better with data, get expenditure reports customised as per your organisational needs. Dissect your payments and create insightful reports with our powerful report builder to generate useful transaction data to act upon. Simplified representation provides actionable insight on data in-depth. This helps reduce leaks and increase overall profitability of the operations



Seamless Auditing Capabilities

Pollux Pay generates appropriate transaction logs and data to assist Corporate Audits sufficiently and appropriately. The. end to end activity tracking module, permanently stored historical transactions assist in ensuring completeness of Corporate Audit.



Two Factor Authentication

All the modules are protected using two-factor authentication for added security. Sensitive information can be protected using this module, where OTPs are generated to initiate/view payments, as per your tailored requirement. This ensures confidentiality, integrity and rightful availability of confidential information..
